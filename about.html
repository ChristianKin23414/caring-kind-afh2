About Caring Kind AFH 2

A small, family-run home where dignity, comfort, and meaningful connection guide every day.

Our mission

Care that feels like family

Our mission is simple: to provide a safe, loving home where adults receive the personal care they need while staying surrounded by the warmth, routines, and small joys that make a house feel like home.

Our story

A home built on love and lived experience

Caring Kind Adult Family Home AFH 2 was founded out of a deeply personal calling: to create the kind of place we'd want for our own parents — quiet, kind, attentive, and full of small kindnesses.

From the very first resident, we set a simple standard: we will know every person by name, every story by heart, and every routine by memory. We will plate meals the way they like them. We'll know which chair is "theirs." We'll remember birthdays, and we'll celebrate them.

Today, that standard is the heart of everything we do.

"Comfort is in the details — a soft blanket, a familiar voice, a kind smile."
What we stand for
